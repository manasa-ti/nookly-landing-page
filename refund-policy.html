Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last Updated: February 2, 2026

1. Subscription Cancellation

You may cancel your Nookly Premium subscription at any time. Please note that:

  • Self-Service Cancellation: Cancellations must be managed through the platform where the purchase was made (e.g., Google Play Store, Apple App Store, or directly via our Web App settings).
  • Access Period: Upon cancellation, you will continue to have access to the premium features until the end of your current billing cycle.
  • No Partial Credits: We do not offer prorated refunds or credits for any unused portion of a subscription period.

2. Refund Policy

All purchases made on Nookly are final and non-refundable. This includes:

  • Subscription fees (Monthly, Quarterly, or Yearly).
  • One-time purchases (e.g., Boosts, Credits, or Virtual Gifts).
  • Unused portions of a subscription.

Exceptions: Refunds will only be considered under the following limited circumstances:

  • Technical Error: If a technical glitch on our end resulted in multiple charges for the same transaction.
  • Legal Mandates: If local laws in your jurisdiction require a mandatory "cooling-off" period or refund window, we will honor those specific legal requirements.

3. Account Termination

If your account is terminated or suspended for violating our Terms of Service or Community Guidelines, you will not be entitled to a refund for any unused subscription time or virtual items.

4. How to Contact Us

If you believe you have been charged in error or have questions regarding your billing, please contact our support team at:

Email: app.nookly@gmail.com

Response Time: We aim to respond to all billing inquiries within 3–5 business days.
