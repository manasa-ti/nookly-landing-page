Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last Updated: February 2, 2026

1. Subscription Cancellation

You may cancel your nookly subscription at any time:

  • Self-service: Cancel via Google Play, App Store, or Web App settings.
  • Access: Premium features remain until the end of your billing cycle.
  • No partial credits: We do not offer prorated refunds for unused time.

2. Refund Policy

All purchases are final and non-refundable, including subscriptions and one-time purchases.

Exceptions: Duplicate charges from a technical error, or where local law requires a refund window.

3. Account Termination

Accounts terminated for Terms or Community Guidelines violations are not eligible for refunds.
